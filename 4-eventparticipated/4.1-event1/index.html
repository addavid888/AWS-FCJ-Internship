Event 1

Summary Report: AWS Cloud Day Vietnam 2025 - AI Edition (Live Telecast & Sessions)

Event Objectives

  • Bring together businesses, builders, and leaders in Vietnam to drive cloud and AI innovation, supporting national digital transformation.
  • Give attendees chances to learn about generative AI, modern cloud tech, and practical digital solutions.
  • Share customer success stories, real use cases, and best practices to help organizations adopt cloud and AI effectively.
  • Create networking opportunities among AWS, partners, startups, and participants to strengthen the innovation ecosystem.

Speakers

  • Eric Yeo, Country General Manager, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos & Myanmar, AWS
  • Dr. Jens Lottner, CEO, Techcombank
  • Ms. Trang Phung, CEO & Co-Founder, U2U Network
  • Jaime Valles, Vice President, General Manager Asia Pacific and Japan, AWS
  • Jun Kai Loke, AI/ML Specialist SA, AWS
  • Kien Nguyen, Solutions Architect, AWS
  • Tamelly Lim, Storage Specialist SA, AWS
  • Binh Tran, Senior Solutions Architect, AWS
  • Taiki Dang, Solutions Architect, AWS

Key Highlights

  • Morning Plenary including opening addresses by senior-level dignitaries and company executives; plus keynotes and customer stories from major Vietnamese organizations.
  • Multiple breakout tracks, such as:
    • Data & Generative AI track — focusing on how organizations use AI/ML, data analytics, and GenAI applications in real business contexts.
    • Migration & Modernization — sessions on migrating to cloud, modernizing legacy applications, embedding GenAI into DevOps workflows, building scalable and resilient infrastructure.

Key Takeaways

  • The event is designed to give both business-level and technical audiences value: whether you’re decision-maker, enterprise leader, startup founder, or developer, there are relevant tracks.
  • Real-world customer stories and cross-industry use cases help bridge the gap between cloud/AI theory and practical business adoption in Vietnam.
  • The structure, from keynote to breakout supports both strategy-level thinking (digital transformation, industry disruption) and ground-level technical learning (migration, GenAI integration, modernization).
  • Networking and directly engaging with AWS experts or partners could unlock collaboration, partnerships, and accelerate innovation for attendees or their organizations.
  • For someone like me as a student, this setup offers a good way to get exposed to how cloud & AI are being used right now in real Vietnamese enterprises.

Event Experience

Attending AWS Cloud Day Vietnam offered me a wide, well-structured experience that combined strategic insights, hands-on learning, and direct exposure to real cloud and AI implementations across industries.

Engaging keynote sessions

  • Senior AWS leaders and industry experts delivered insights on Vietnam’s digital transformation, cloud adoption trends, and the real impact of generative AI.
  • Customer success stories from major enterprises provided concrete examples of data modernization and AI-driven innovation at scale.

Practical exposure through demos

  • AWS engineers and partners provided direct explanations of architectures, letting me connect high-level ideas to actual implementations.

Community interaction and networking

  • Conversations with engineers, startup founders, and tech leads helped me see how different teams approach problem-solving with cloud services.
  • These exchanges made it clearer how cloud adoption varies between industries, and how teams bridge gaps between business goals and technical strategies.

Key reflections

  • Seeing real-world Vietnamese use cases made the concepts more grounded and easier to connect with.
  • The mix of strategic talks, practical demos, and hands-on labs created a balanced picture of what it takes to build, scale, and modernize systems on AWS today.
