### Event Objectives

- Introduce participants to the fundamentals of Generative AI and the AWS services that support modern AI development.
- Provide a clear understanding of foundational models and Amazon Bedrock's capabilities for real-world applications.
- Equip students and developers with practical knowledge of prompt engineering, RAG workflows, and scalable AI architecture patterns.
- Encourage hands-on engagement and learning through interactive activities such as the Kahoot knowledge quiz.

### Speakers

- Dinh Le Hoang Anh - Cloud Engineer Trainee, First Cloud AI Journey
- Lam Tuan Kiet - Sr DevOps Engineer, FPT Software
- Danh Hoang Hieu Nghi - AI Engineer Renova Cloud

### Key Highlights

- Overview of foundational GenAI concepts and how they are implemented on AWS, with a strong focus on Amazon Bedrock.
- Detailed explanation of the process of building production-grade AI agents, including orchestration with Bedrock AgentCore.
- Deep dive into prompt engineering techniques and methods to reduce hallucination for more reliable AI outputs.
- Exploration of RAG workflows covering data ingestion, embedding generation, and vector database integration.
- Showcases of practical applications such as chatbots and optical search.
- High attendee engagement, with over 200 students and AWS Cloud Club members creating an energetic learning environment.
- A closing Kahoot mini-game to reinforce key concepts from the session.

### Key Takeaways

- Amazon Bedrock provides a robust platform for building, scaling, and operationalizing generative AI applications.
- Moving from prototype to production requires structured workflows, including agent orchestration, retrieval layers, and evaluation steps.
- Prompt quality, context retrieval, and vector search are critical to reducing hallucination and improving output reliability.
- The AWS AI ecosystem supports developers of all backgrounds, making advanced AI development more accessible and practical.

### Event Experience

- This brought together a large and energetic audience, creating a collaborative space to explore generative AI from multiple angles. The session provided a clear breakdown of how foundational models operate and how Amazon Bedrock simplifies the development and deployment of AI solutions.
- Hands-on demonstrations and architectural walkthroughs made the concepts feel grounded and applicable. Seeing real examples of chatbot implementation and summarization workflows helped connect theory to actual implementation.
- The event maintained strong engagement throughout, and the Kahoot mini-game offered a fun, interactive way for participants to test how much they had absorbed.
- Overall, the session delivered a meaningful blend of technical insight, real use cases, and hands-on learning, making it a valuable experience for students and aspiring AI builders.
